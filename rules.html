Rules & Scoring

Complete Gamification Guide

Get ready to play, participate, and power up your experience. This guide shows you exactly how to score big and rise to the top of the leaderboard.

This document consolidates all gamification categories, rules, and scoring across the Eprenz Summit, Eprenz Club (Mighty Networks), Event Registration, and Deal Room participation.

Categories at a Glance

  1. Event Registration
  2. Summit Participation (Backstage Platform)
  3. Eprenz Club Engagement (Mighty Networks)
  4. Bonus Points
  5. Revenue Share Program (Cash Incentives)

1. Event Registration

Rewarding you for driving people to the summit.

  • 1 point per free ticket registered
  • 2 points per $1 generated from paid ticket sales + revenue sharing

2. Eprenz Club Engagement (Mighty Networks)

Points for engaging with the Eprenz Club community.

  • 1 point per total contribution
  • 1 point per visit
  • 1 point per comment
  • 1 point per reaction

3. Summit Participation (Backstage Platform)

Earn points for setting up your profile, joining sessions, networking, and engaging with sponsors.

a. Profile & Event Setup

  • Upload Profile Image – 10 pts (max 1)
  • Add Profile Bio – 10 pts (max 1)
  • Upload a Photo – 10 pts (max 5)

b. Session Participation

  • Join a Session – 20 pts (no max)
  • Engage in Session Q&A – 5 pts (no max)
  • Engage in Session Polls – 5 pts (no max)
  • Engage in Session Chat – 5 pts (no max)
  • Share Session Feedback – 10 pts (max 12)

c. Networking Engagement

  • Enter Virtual Lounge – 20 pts (max 6)
  • Meet with Attendee – 10 pts (max 10)
  • Engage in Discussion Channel – 10 pts (max 5)

d. Exhibitor / Sponsor Engagement

  • Meet with Exhibitor – 20 pts (max 5)
  • Submit Exhibitor Inquiry – 20 pts (max 15)
  • Add Exhibitor to Favorites – 20 pts (max 5)

e. Event-wide Participation

  • Engage in Event Poll – 10 pts (max 14)
  • Comment on Event Announcement – 10 pts (max 10)
  • Post on Social Wall – 20 pts (max 10)
  • Comment on Social Wall – 20 pts (max 10)
  • Like a Photo in the Gallery – 20 pts (max 5)

4. Deal Room Participation

For driving real business outcomes through the Deal Room.

  • 25 pts per unique code submitted
  • 1 pt per $1 of purchases
  • +25 bonus pts for any purchase
  • +50 bonus pts for completing all deal codes

5. Bonus Points

Extra rewards for high-value contributions and commitment.

  • Sponsor warm referral – +50 pts
  • Closed deal via sponsor referral – +250 pts
  • Attend both summit days – +40 pts

6. Revenue Share Program (Cash)

In addition to points, participants can earn real revenue share.

Participants earn 20% revenue share on:

  • Summit ticket sales made via their referral link
  • Sponsor warm referral closings (you introduce, Eprenz completes the deal)
